Prarang is a mobile application that reaches out to people in their own cities/regions , in their own language with photo- centered content which is meaningful, re-connects them with their roots adds colour positivity to their daily life. The content is delivered in the form of letters or daily-posts, which are then re-organized into a 60-topic grid for each city/region. Life in each geography is a balance of Culture Nature, with 3 categories 30 sub-categories under each, totalling a Prarang- Sixty. Each user can also save these daily posts, comment on them share them with friends family who don’t have a mobile phone. City Citizenship based on “Respect for Diversity, A Belief in Unity”.– Cities with I-RULE : Internet – Localized, socially-responsible internet content for each city in its local language. Research – Research based on evolutionary data and science with a focus on economic geography (not on political history and people/dynasties) & employment. Urbanization – Bio-regional City Planning - Interdependence of Culture and Nature. Language – Diversity prioritized by language (and not by political geography nor ethnicity). Education – Trans-disciplinary, value-embedding and holistic education. Creating curiosity for lifelong learning & continuous skill upgrade.
